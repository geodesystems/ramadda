Section 2.3: Multiple Views of Content
RAMADDA provides a number of "Views" of the content.
  • You can view the entries of a folder as a calender, timeline, map, etc.
  • These are available through the View menu and through the icons in the toolbar.
  • A number of non-html views are also provided through the Connect menu - RSS, ATOM, JSON, THREDDS Catalog, etc.

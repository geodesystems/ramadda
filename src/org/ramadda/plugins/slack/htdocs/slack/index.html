Integrating Slack and RAMADDA

Slack is a powerful group communication platform and provides the ability to integrate external services. You can integrate Slack with RAMADDA and create wiki pages, blog posts and notes in RAMADDA from Slack as well as search and navigate through your RAMADDA content.
  • Import RAMADDA content into Slack with the Slack Shell.
  • Create RAMADDA blog posts and wiki pages directly from Slack
  • Import and display Slack stuff in RAMADDA.
  • Publish events and files automatically from RAMADDA to Slack.

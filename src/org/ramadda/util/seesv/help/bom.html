If you ever have a problem where the first column name looks like, e.g.: some_name Joe Sue And you are referencing some_name seesv:seesv -columns some_name and you get an error like: 
ColumnSelector: Could not find column:some_name
possible columns: [some_name, 0]
Then it could be that there is a leading bom character. In that case call: seesv:seesv -bom -columns some_name
